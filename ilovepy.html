About

Task

The primary task of our website for the traditional food restaurant and store is to provide a seamless and immersive online platform that captures the essence of Paraguayan culinary heritage. Ultimately, our website aims to drive traffic to our physical restaurant and increase online sales for our store.We want to inspire diners to visit I love Paraguay and experience the warmth of hospitality firsthand.

Solution

The website will feature an intuitive layout that allows for seamless navigation, enabling users to browse, select, and learn about offerings effortlessly.In the store section, website will offer a user-friendly interface that simplifies the shopping experience for our valued customers.Interactive elements such as customer reviews will allow to gather valuable insights and continuously improve offerings.

bright colors

We chose vibrant colors for the website to capture the lively and colorful essence of Paraguayan culture and cuisine. The bright hues reflect the energy, warmth, and joy that are integral to our traditional dishes and celebrations. By using vibrant colors, we aim to evoke a sense of excitement and adventure, inviting visitors to immerse themselves in the vibrant flavors and rich traditions of Paraguay.

Site structure

We divided the website structure into Restaurant and Store sections to provide a clear and organized experience for visitors. By separating the two sections, we aim to: - provide a seamless and focused experience for those looking to dine at our restaurant - offer a convenient platform for customers to purchase our traditional products online.
