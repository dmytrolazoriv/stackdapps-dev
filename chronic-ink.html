About

Task

The main task of a website for a tattoo studio is to create trust between the guest and the master, to answer all the user's questions and to provide an appropriate selection of sketches, care products or services.We want the person who visits the site to be able to get all the information on his own, to understand the level of safety of the procedures and the skill of the artists.

Solution

It's crucial to feature individual artist portfolios, allowing clients to browse through each artist's unique style, specialties, and previous works.Including user-friendly booking forms and contact information allows get in touch with the studio effortlessly.Displaying client testimonials can help build trust and credibility, showcasing the studio's reputation for exceptional artistry and customer satisfaction.

Dark gray is a versatile color that pairs well with a range of other hues, allowing the vibrant colors of tattoos to stand out while maintaining a cohesive and harmonious look.Beige adds warmth and comfort to the design, creating a welcoming and inviting atmosphere for visitors to the website. Also, beige resembles human skin, which is the main area of the studio's work.
